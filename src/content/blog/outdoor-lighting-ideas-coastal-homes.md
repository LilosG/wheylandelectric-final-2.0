## Making the Most of Outdoor Living in San Diego

San Diego's mild climate means outdoor spaces are used year-round. The right outdoor lighting extends your living space into the evening while improving safety, security, and curb appeal.

## Security Lighting

Well-placed security lighting deters intruders and helps you navigate your property safely after dark. Options include:

- **Motion-activated floodlights** at entry points and along driveways
- **Dusk-to-dawn fixtures** at garage doors and side gates
- **Low-voltage pathway lights** along walkways and steps

## Landscape and Accent Lighting

Landscape lighting highlights your home's architecture and outdoor features:

- **Uplighting** on trees, columns, or textured walls
- **Downlighting** from eaves or pergolas for a natural moonlight effect
- **Step and deck lights** for safety on stairs and elevated surfaces
- **Garden path lights** to define walkways through landscaping

## Outdoor Living Space Lighting

For patios, pergolas, and outdoor kitchens:

- **String lights or pendant fixtures** over dining and seating areas
- **Recessed can lights** under pergola or patio cover rooflines
- **Ceiling fans with integrated lights** for covered spaces
- **Infratech heaters with lighting** for cool evening comfort

## Coastal Considerations

San Diego's coastal environments require attention to:

- **Corrosion-resistant fixtures** rated for salt air exposure
- **Marine-grade mounting hardware** within a few miles of the coast
- **Proper weatherproofing** of all outdoor electrical connections
- **Dark sky compliance** in communities that require it

## LED Is the Standard

LED outdoor lighting is now the standard for efficiency and longevity. Benefits include:

- Lower energy consumption (up to 80% less than halogen)
- 25,000–50,000 hour lifespan
- Available in warm to cool color temperatures
- Dimmable options for ambiance control

## Professional Installation Matters

Outdoor electrical work requires weatherproof connections, proper circuit protection, and code-compliant installation. A licensed electrician ensures your outdoor lighting is safe, durable, and installed to last.
