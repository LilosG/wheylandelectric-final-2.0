# Local Addenda -- Assets Needed per City & Service

> Lists the local assets, unique content, and supporting materials needed to make each city page and money page substantive and non-duplicative.

---

## 1. Per-City Asset Requirements

Each of the 7 Wave 1 city landing pages requires these local assets:

| Asset | Description | Source |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| `localIntroSeed` | Genuinely unique city intro paragraph (150-250 words) | Data file |
| Neighborhood list | 8-15 neighborhoods/communities within the city | Research / local knowledge |
| ZIP codes | All ZIP codes within city limits | USPS / city data |
| Housing stock notes | Age ranges, common panel types, wiring issues | Local knowledge / building dept |
| Permit process notes | City-specific permit requirements, fees, timeline | City building department |
| Local landmarks | 2-4 recognizable landmarks for context | Local knowledge |
| Testimonial/review | At least 1 customer review from this city | Customer database |
| Hero image | City-relevant photo (ideally completed project) | Photo library |
| Neighboring cities | 2-3 adjacent cities for cross-linking | Geography |

## 4. Money Page Uniqueness Checklist

Each of the 56 money pages (`/{city}/{service}/`) must have:

### Minimum 3 of 6 City-Specific Elements

| # | Element | Example |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | Neighborhood callouts | "Serving Leucadia, Cardiff, and Olivenhain" |
| 2 | City permit/inspection reference | "Del Mar requires permits per the CDD" |
| 3 | Housing stock detail | "Fire Mountain homes often have 100A panels" |
| 4 | Proximity/geography note | "Based in Carlsbad, 10 minutes from Encinitas" |
| 5 | Local testimonial or case study | "Recently installed 3 EV chargers at RSF estate" |
| 6 | City-specific pricing context | "RSF estates often need 400A service" |

### Minimum 3 of 4 Service-Specific Elements

| # | Element | Example |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | Process steps unique to service | "Step 1: Panel evaluation..." |
| 2 | Service-specific FAQs (not recycled) | "How long does panel upgrade take in {city}?" |
| 3 | Before/after or project example | Photo or narrative |
| 4 | Code/permit requirements for service + city | "NEC 625.40 requires dedicated EV circuit" |

### Uniqueness Source

Money page unique content comes from `localizedIntroSeedByCity` entries in the service data files. Each city x service combination has a genuinely distinct seed paragraph. Combined with city-specific FAQ answers, this ensures no two money pages share more than 40% body content.
