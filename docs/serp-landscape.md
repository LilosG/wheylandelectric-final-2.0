## 4. Competitor Profiles

### Competitor A -- Typical Local Electrician

- **Website:** 5-15 pages total
- **City pages:** 0-1 (usually just a service-area mention)
- **Service pages:** 1 generic "services" page listing everything
- **Content depth:** Thin (under 300 words per page)
- **Reviews:** 20-80 on Google
- **Weakness:** No city-specific content, no service-specific pages, minimal internal linking

### Competitor B -- Well-Optimized Local Electrician

- **Website:** 20-40 pages
- **City pages:** 3-5 city pages (often thin, boilerplate swapped)
- **Service pages:** 5-10 dedicated service pages
- **Content depth:** Moderate (300-600 words)
- **Reviews:** 80-200+ on Google
- **Weakness:** City pages are often doorway-style with swapped city names, limited unique content

### Competitor C -- Lead-Gen / Directory (Yelp, Angi, Thumbtack)

- **Website:** Massive domain authority
- **City pages:** Auto-generated for every city
- **Content depth:** Thin but high authority
- **Weakness:** No real expertise content, cannot match E-E-A-T signals of a real electrician's site

### Wheyland Electric Advantage

1. **88 pages at launch** vs. competitors' 5-40 pages
2. **Dedicated city x service money pages** that no local competitor has
3. **Rich local content** (neighborhoods, permits, building codes) vs. competitors' boilerplate
4. **Strong internal linking architecture** vs. competitors' flat or broken link structures
5. **Astro static site = fast Core Web Vitals** vs. competitors' slow WordPress sites

## 6. Local Pack Strategy

The Local Pack (map 3-pack) is the most valuable SERP real estate for local electrician queries. Organic rankings support but do not replace Local Pack visibility.

### Key Local Pack Ranking Factors

1. **GBP proximity** -- physical address relative to searcher
2. **GBP relevance** -- category, description, services listed
3. **GBP prominence** -- review count, review rating, citations
4. **Website signals** -- city page links from GBP, on-page city relevance

### Recommendations

- Ensure GBP primary category is "Electrician"
- Add all 8 Wave 1 services as GBP services
- Link GBP website field to homepage; use GBP "appointment" link for `/free-estimates/`
- Maintain 4.5+ star rating; respond to all reviews within 48 hours
- Post weekly GBP updates featuring recent projects in different cities
