| Average Rating | 5.0 | Maintain 4.8+ |
| Response Rate | Verify | 100% -- respond to every review |
| Response Time | Verify | Within 48 hours |

### Review Generation Tactics

1. **Post-job follow-up.** Send review request email/text within 24 hours of job completion with direct Google review link.
2. **Review link placement:** Thank-you page (`/thank-you/`), email signatures, invoice follow-up emails.
3. **Never incentivize.** No discounts or gifts for reviews -- violates Google policy.

### Review Response Templates

**Positive:**
> Thank you, {Name}! We are glad the {service} went smoothly at your {City} home. We appreciate you sharing your experience.

**Negative:**
> {Name}, we are sorry to hear about your experience. This does not reflect our standards. Please call us at (858) 663-5564 so we can discuss this directly.

### Review Keyword Reinforcement
When responding, naturally incorporate keywords: "EV charger installation," "panel upgrade," "Carlsbad," "electrician." This reinforces relevance signals.
