## Process Steps

1. **Assessment** -- We evaluate your electrical panel capacity, determine the optimal charger location in your garage or driveway area, and review your vehicle's charging requirements. Many Carlsbad homes in La Costa and Aviara built after 2000 have 200-amp panels with room for an EV circuit. Older homes in the Village or Barrio may require a panel evaluation first.

2. **Recommendation** -- Based on the assessment, we recommend the best charger type (hardwired vs. plug-in) and circuit configuration for your home. We consider your vehicle, daily driving distance, and whether you plan to add a second EV.

3. **Permitting** -- We pull all required permits with the City of Carlsbad and coordinate inspection scheduling. California's AB 1236 streamlined EV charger permitting, and Carlsbad follows this process. Permit fees are typically $100-$200.

4. **Installation** -- Our licensed electricians install the dedicated circuit from your electrical panel to the charger location. This includes running conduit and wiring, installing a dedicated breaker, mounting the charger, and making all connections to code.

5. **Testing & Handoff** -- We test the charger with your vehicle, verify proper operation at full amperage, walk you through the controls, and schedule the City of Carlsbad final inspection.

## FAQs (6)

**Q: How much does EV charger installation cost in Carlsbad?**
A: Most Carlsbad installations range from $500 to $2,500 depending on the distance from your panel to the charger location and whether a panel upgrade is needed. We provide a free written estimate covering labor, materials, and permitting so you know the cost before any work begins.

**Q: Are you licensed to perform EV charger installation in Carlsbad?**
A: Yes. Wheyland Electric holds California C-10 Electrical Contractor License #940291 and is fully bonded and insured. We have been serving Carlsbad since 2008 and handle all City of Carlsbad permitting and inspections.

**Q: What type of charger do I need for my electric vehicle?**
A: For home use, we recommend a Level 2 (240V) charger, which adds 25-40 miles of range per hour. For Tesla owners, the Tesla Wall Connector is ideal. For other EVs, the ChargePoint Home Flex or Grizzl-E are excellent universal options. We help you choose the right charger during our assessment.

**Q: How long does a Level 2 EV charger installation take?**
A: The on-site installation typically takes 2-4 hours. The permit process with the City of Carlsbad adds 3-5 business days. From your initial call to charging your EV, expect about 1-2 weeks total.

**Q: Do I need a panel upgrade before installing an EV charger?**
A: Not always. If your panel is 200 amps with available breaker space, we can usually add an EV circuit without an upgrade. If your panel is 100 amps or full, a panel upgrade to 200 amps may be needed. We perform a load calculation during our assessment to determine exactly what your Carlsbad home needs.

**Q: Can you install an EV charger outdoors or in a driveway?**
A: Yes. If your EV parks in a driveway rather than a garage, we install outdoor-rated chargers (NEMA 4 enclosure) on an exterior wall. We run conduit neatly along the structure for a clean, code-compliant installation that handles Carlsbad's coastal weather.
